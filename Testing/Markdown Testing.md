### Lists
Unordered lists use hyphen and space (or asterisk or plus sign)
- Something
- something else
- more
+ list item
+ more list items
* list item
* another

Ordered lists begin with numbers and periods followed by a space
1. Find Sarah Connor
2. Find John Conner
3. Get to the Choppa
